4. Technical Analysis
Splunk SIEM detected Windows Security EventCode 4720 indicating 
a new local user account was created. The account named 
"HackerBackdoor" was created by SOCUser on DESKTOP-HEC7FEF. 
This is a classic persistence technique used by attackers after 
gaining initial access to maintain long-term presence on a system 
even if their primary access method is discovered and removed.

**Key Observations:**
- Account name "HackerBackdoor" is highly suspicious
- Created by SOCUser — possible compromised admin account
- Account creation occurred outside normal business hours
- No legitimate business justification for the account
- Account was created with a strong password — indicates planning

10. Lessons Learned
This incident demonstrates the critical importance of monitoring 
Windows EventCode 4720 in real-time. Backdoor account creation is 
one of the most common persistence techniques used by attackers 
post-compromise. Early detection via SIEM significantly reduces 
dwell time and limits the attacker's ability to maintain access.
The MITRE ATT&CK framework mapping helped quickly categorize the 
threat and prioritize the response.
